# Molecules

Simple groups of UI elements functioning together as a unit. Molecules are composed of **2-3 atoms** combined with layout logic.

## üéØ What Are Molecules?

Molecules are the **second level** of atomic design:

- ‚úÖ Combine 2-3 atoms into functional groups
- ‚úÖ Have simple, focused purpose
- ‚úÖ Reusable across different contexts
- ‚úÖ No complex business logic
- ‚úÖ Layout and composition only
