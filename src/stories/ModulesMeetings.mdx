## ðŸ“¦ Components

### Meeting Cards

- **MeetingCard**: Full meeting card with details, agenda, attendees
- **TodayMeetingCard**: Compact card for today's meetings (dashboard)

### Agenda Management

- **AgendaItemView**: View and edit individual agenda items
- **AgendaItemEditor**: Rich text editor for agenda notes

### Action Items & Decisions

- **ActionItemsList**: List of action items with create/edit/delete
- **DecisionsList**: List of decisions made during meeting

### Meeting Creation

- **CreateMeetingModal**: Modal for creating new meetings
- **AttendeeSelector**: Multi-select for choosing attendees
- **SecretarySelector**: Select meeting secretary
- **SecretaryConfirmationDialog**: Confirm secretary assignment
