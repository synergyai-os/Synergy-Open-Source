### [Atoms](?path=/docs/design-system-atoms--docs)

Single interactive elements with design tokens applied.

**Examples**: Button, Input, Badge, Card, Icon, Avatar  
**Location**: `src/lib/components/atoms/`

### [Molecules](?path=/docs/design-system-molecules--docs)

2-3 atoms composed together with layout logic.

**Examples**: FormField (Label + Input + Error), SearchBar  
**Location**: `src/lib/components/molecules/`

### [Organisms](?path=/docs/design-system-organisms--docs)

Complex sections combining multiple atoms and molecules.

**Examples**: Dialog, Modal, Command Palette  
**Location**: `src/lib/components/organisms/`

### [Templates](?path=/docs/design-system-templates--docs)

Page-level layouts with placeholder content.

**Examples**: AuthLayout, DashboardLayout, SettingsLayout  
**Location**: `src/lib/components/templates/`

### [Pages](?path=/docs/design-system-pages--docs)

Templates filled with real content and data.

**Examples**: LoginPage, DashboardPage, SettingsPage  
**Location**: `src/routes/`
