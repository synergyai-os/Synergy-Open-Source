## ðŸ“¦ Components

### Visualization

- **OrgChart**: Interactive nested bubble chart showing organizational hierarchy
- **CategoryHeader**: Header for circle/role categories

### Detail Panels

- **CircleDetailPanel**: Panel showing circle details, members, roles
- **RoleDetailPanel**: Panel showing role details, responsibilities
- **CircleMembersPanel**: List of circle members with management
- **CircleRolesPanel**: List of roles in a circle

### Headers & Navigation

- **CircleDetailHeader**: Header for circle detail view
- **RoleDetailHeader**: Header for role detail view
- **PanelBreadcrumbBar**: Breadcrumb navigation for panel hierarchy

### Management

- **RoleCard**: Card displaying role information
