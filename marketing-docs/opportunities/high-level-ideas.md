### Why

- **Control**: Users own their data
- **Compliance**: GDPR, SOC 2, HIPAA-ready

- **Encryption**: Data encrypted at rest and in transit
- **Open source**: Audit the code, trust the system

**Deployment Options**:
1. Cloud-hosted (managed by us)
2. Self-hosted (AWS, Azure, on-prem)
3. Hybrid (some data self-hosted, some cloud)

### Success Signals

- âœ… 50%+ customers choose self-hosted (vs. cloud)

### Dependencies
